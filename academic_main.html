Academia

I was a PhD Student in the School of Mathematics and Statistics from September 2016. I submitted my thesis, entitled "Enuemrating 0-simple semigroups", in October 2020 and passed my viva subject to minor corrections in January 2021. My research is in the area of Computational Semigroup Theory and my supervisor was Dr James D. Mitchell . I also completed my undergraduate degree in St Andrews, graduating with a first class MMath degree in 2016. The title of my masters project was Constructing a Database of Primitive Permutation Groups and it was supervised by Dr Colva M Roney-Dougal .

I have contributed to the computational algebra system GAP as well as making contrubtions to two of its packages: Semigroups and Digraphs. As part of my PhD Thesis I wrote GAP code that can count or generate a database of various classes of 0-simple semigroups.
